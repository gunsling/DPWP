Magnets

My first design of the name tags I glued the magnets into the name tag. This is an expensive way of doing it. Now if I am going to use the magnet route it is much cheaper to glue in metallic washers. Down side it the magnet bar (in the picture) tends to break fairly easy.

Bar pin

The bar pin seems to be the way to go. Cheap and effective.

Ironing

Ironing can change the finish and smooth it out or change colors. On the wood PLA it can add a burn to the wood. Can add an hour to a single print.
