The Brother QL-500 label printer can only print on brother labels do to a photo sensor that looks for a black rectangle on the back of the labels. The labels also come on a reel that has punched out holes that trigger a sensors in the machine that tells the machine (and computer) about the media (label size and details). I have generic thermo labels, this takes a reel of printed black and white rectangles, clear tape and generic labelsm which will make them all into one, with a 3d printed sensor trigger so I can print on generic labels. https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3683750

More of my Brother QL-500 Generic Label hack https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3683750
